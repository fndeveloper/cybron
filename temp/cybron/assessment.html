Assessment & Methodology

You are the good assessors of your goodself

A Unique Marking & Assessment Methodology

Our assessment is not to judge your skills only, we would like to develop your positive mindsets with the cyber security skills and techniques.

We are assessing the candidates through some diagnostics, and then some formative questions. The system will be completly online and we will provide the access to our partners assessment center, who can take the candidate exams. On the other hand, if the school, college, university does not have the capacity to take online then physical arrangements can be done for assessment and examinations.
