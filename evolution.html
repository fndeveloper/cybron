1997
The Founder of Cybron

The beginning of Cybron was laid by one person Mr. Muhammad Shaheer Waqar (PhD AI and Cyber Security): Back in 1997, this passionate guy, whose major is Electronics and Information Technology, had involved in the research and real-time consulting services. He has been served public and private sector and developed the entire framework of Cybron, which facilitates the youth as well as the professional

2005
Cybron Consortium

Cybron - Cyber Security Rapid Offensive Native Forum develops the consortium of professionals and start discussing about various aspect of cyber security threats and challenges on different platorms at various countries. The Researcher Mr. M. Shaheer Waqar evolves the idea and establish the learning framework for students & professionals

2023
Cybron Framework Publishing

The Cybron were established in 1997, but the final version of the framework has been published in 11th Jan 2023 and first time in the world and the curriculum and syllabus are available for kids and youth from grassroot level. The Cybron can be a part of every school, where it can be plugged with any school curriculum and can also be enabled in intermediate and university level as well.
